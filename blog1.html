ABOUT UNSECURED BUSINESS FINANCING

Unsecured business funds for start-ups

✔ Fast processing and execution: In the case of secured loans, banks have to verify the value of the collateral provided. This exercise often takes more time. However, with an unsecured loan, the lender does not have to appraise the value of collateral or documents such as sales transactions or credit reports.

✔ Useful in crises : In a sudden business emergency, unsecured business loans are one of the best and fastest financing options. Business loans of up to Rs 75 lakh are disbursed within 48 hours, ensuring that your business never stops due to lack of funds.

✔ Improve your credit score : Taking an unsecured business loan and repaying the EMIs on time will improve your credit score. As your credit score improves, so do your chances of getting more loans.

✔ Flexibility: Unsecured business loans can be obtained and repaid on terms that suit you. You can borrow as much as you need and repay part without penalty.

✔ No Asset Required: For new businesses that do not have significant assets to secure the loan, unsecured loans are a better option. These companies may have good cash flow and credit scores, but because they are new, they may not have many assets. Therefore, unsecured loans are a sure way to meet your financial needs.

✔ No risk to assets: When applying for a secured loan, long-term non-payment may put the secured assets at risk. Lenders have the option to recover loan defaults through assets. However, with an unsecured business loan, your assets are not exposed to such risks.
